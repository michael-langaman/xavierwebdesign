Let's Go On An Adventure!


A game company is running tests for video game simulators. Fortunately, you are chosen as one of the game-testers. If you win the game, you are rewarded the simulator for free. The game starts, and in order to win you must fend off the wave of beastial creatures and destroy the leader. To help you in your journey, you are able to choose between to sets of weapons: Long Range Attack or Short Range Attack.
